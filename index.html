About Us

Initiative for Learning Disabilities Kenya (ILDK) is a parent-led charity registered in Kenya comprising parents, caregivers, siblings, and persons living with multiple disabilities and partners. The organization aims to provide a platform for stakeholder engagement and to promote efforts that address challenges for persons living with multiple disabilities.

ILDK is dedicated to being a voice for the voiceless and a champion for persons living with multiple disabilities through community engagement that creates access to education and healthcare.
